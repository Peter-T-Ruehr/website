FishEgger: A fish egg pressure resistance test stand

A small device to measure break resistance of sample - originally designed to test fish egg pressure resistance. With live video feed and control and logging software.
Built in 2025 for the Leibniz Institute for the Analysis of Biodiversity Change (LIB), Germany.
