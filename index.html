About Peter and this Page

Welcome to my data science portfolio webpage! As an entomologist, evolutionary biologist, and researcher in biomechanics, I am passionate about using data-driven approaches to solve complex problems and uncover valuable insights. In this portfolio, you will find a collection of selected projects that showcase my skills and expertise in various areas of data science, including data analysis, machine learning, data visualization, and more. With a strong background in the natural sciences, I bring a unique perspective to data science and strive to integrate biological knowledge with cutting-edge data analysis techniques. Whether you are a fellow (data) scientist or simply someone interested in the power of data, I invite you to explore my work and see how data can transform our understanding of the natural world. You may also find my scientific CV and a page to contact me.
