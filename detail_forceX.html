forceX: A mobile bite force measurement setup

A 3D-printable setup to reliably and repeatably measure bite forces of small and large insects with a piezoelectric force sensor. With this device I measured bite forces of about 650 insect species of all sizes betwen between 3 mm and 180 cm.
