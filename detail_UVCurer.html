UV Curing Device: A setup that allows attaching und UV-curing with both hands.

A setup used to cure UV glue for tiny sample preparation. The foot pedal allows both hands to be free when attaching the specimen and curing the glue.
Built in 2024 for the University of Bonn, Germany.
